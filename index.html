Our Story

Rooted in the Uttarakhand Tradition

Ashish Trading Company, led by Ashish Sharma, brings you the finest jaggery sourced directly from traditional farms nestled in the Himalayan foothills of Uttarakhand. Every block and every grain carries the essence of pure, unadulterated sugarcane.

We believe in preserving the time-honoured methods of jaggery making — slow-cooking the sugarcane juice in iron vessels, free from artificial additives, chemical bleaching, or preservatives. What you get is jaggery just as nature intended.

What We Offer

Our Products

Two varieties of premium Uttarakhand jaggery — each crafted with care, suited for different culinary and commercial uses.

Traditional Jaggery

Classic block-form jaggery made from fresh sugarcane juice using traditional open-pan methods. Rich amber colour, natural sweetness with earthy undertones — perfect for sweets, cooking, and ayurvedic use.

Block Form Deep Flavour Cooking Ayurvedic

Powdered Jaggery

Finely milled jaggery powder — convenient, easy to dissolve, and versatile. Ideal for baking, beverages, health drinks, and modern kitchens that prefer a quick-dissolving natural sweetener over refined sugar.

Powdered Easy Dissolve Baking Beverages
