The Process

How Our Jaggary is Made

Every batch follows a centuries-old tradition — from the sugarcane field to your table, with zero shortcuts and zero chemicals.

1

Harvest

Fresh sugarcane is hand-cut from local Uttarakhand farms at peak ripeness, when natural sugar content is highest.

2

Press & Extract

Sugarcane is crushed in traditional iron presses to extract pure, fresh juice — no solvents, no chemicals involved.

3

Slow Cook

Juice is slow-cooked in open iron pans over natural heat. Constant stirring ensures even concentration without burning.

4

Set & Pack

Thickened syrup is poured into moulds or dried into powder. Set naturally, packed fresh — pure jaggery ready to deliver.

What is the minimum order quantity?
We accept orders starting from 10 kg for individual buyers. For bulk wholesale pricing, we recommend orders of 50 kg or more. Please mention your requirement in the enquiry form and we'll share the best pricing.
Do you ship outside Uttarakhand?
Yes, we supply across India. We work with trusted logistics partners to ensure safe, timely delivery of your jaggery order. Shipping charges depend on quantity and destination — contact us for a quote.
Is your jaggery FSSAI certified?
Our products are produced under food-safe conditions in compliance with FSSAI guidelines. Please reach out for documentation and quality details relevant to your business needs.
What is the shelf life of your jaggery?
Traditional block jaggery has a shelf life of 6–12 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from moisture. Powdered jaggery lasts 3–6 months under similar conditions. Airtight packaging significantly extends freshness.
Can I get custom packaging for my brand?
Yes! We offer white-label and custom packaging options for retailers and brand owners. Get in touch via the enquiry form with your branding requirements and quantity, and we'll discuss the options available.
What's the difference between traditional block and powdered jaggery?
Traditional block jaggery is set into solid cakes after cooking — great for sweets, cooking, and ayurvedic use. Powdered jaggery is the same product finely milled for easy dissolving — perfect for beverages, baking, and health drinks. Both have identical nutritional value.
